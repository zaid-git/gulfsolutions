About Us

GulfSolutions was founded in 2014 with the mission of helping doctors and medical professionals in the UAE achieve their continuing medical education requirement with as much ease as possible. At GulfSolutions our expertise is two-fold. GulfConferenceSolutions organizes highly relevent and interesting conferences while guarenteeing the lowest possible price. GulfDesignSolutions is a design firm that creates websites, graphics, videos and handles production for almost all forms of media. Both of our firms work in conjunction to provide the most interesting conferences possible.
